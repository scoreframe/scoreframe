Become a Beta Tester

Help shape ScoreFrame before the public launch. Try new features first, report bugs, and tell us what to build next.

1

Join the Google Group

Google Play requires beta testers to be in a Google Group. This takes 10 seconds — just click the button below and hit "Join group." You'll use the same Google account that you use on the Play Store.

Why a Google Group? Google Play's closed testing track requires testers to be part of a group. This is how Google knows you're authorized to download the beta. You won't receive any emails from the group — it's just for access.
Join ScoreFrame Beta Group
2

Opt In on Google Play

After joining the group, click the link below to opt in to the ScoreFrame testing program on Google Play. This tells the Play Store to show you the beta version of the app.

Note: It can take a few minutes after joining the Google Group for the opt-in link to work. If you see "not available," wait 5 minutes and try again.
Opt In to Testing
3

Install ScoreFrame

Once you've opted in, ScoreFrame will appear in the Google Play Store like any other app. Download it and you're in.

Open in Google Play
4

Try It Out & Give Feedback

Use ScoreFrame at your next game, bowling night, or even just around the house. Then tell us what you think — what works, what's confusing, what's missing, what broke.
