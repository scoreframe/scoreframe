Privacy Policy

Effective March 5, 2026 | Last updated March 5, 2026

Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

ScoreFrame does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All recordings stay on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud uploads, and no tracking.

This Privacy Policy describes how ScoreFrame handles information when you use our Android application. We built ScoreFrame to help you record sports video with professional scoreboard overlays. Your data stays yours.

Permissions

Permissions We Request and Why

ScoreFrame requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Camera — Records video footage and displays a live viewfinder with scoreboard overlay. Video is processed on-device using OpenGL ES and saved directly to local storage.
  • Microphone — Captures game audio alongside video. A mute toggle is available. Audio is never transmitted externally.
  • Storage — Saves recorded video files, highlight clips, and exported stats PDFs to your device.
  • Wi-Fi / Network State — Used for remote scoring over Wi-Fi Direct. This is peer-to-peer between your devices, with no external server involved.
  • Nearby Devices / Wi-Fi Direct — Discovers and connects to a second phone for remote scoring. The connection is local and direct.
  • Modify Audio Settings — Used for the microphone mute feature during recording.
  • Internet Access — Used only for advertisements (Google AdMob) on the free tier and for in-app purchases (Google Play Billing). ScoreFrame does not upload any of your content or personal data.
  • Vibration — Haptic feedback on scoring actions and external button events.

Permissions are requested only when you first use the relevant feature and are never used beyond what is described above.

Data Collection

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information (ScoreFrame has no accounts)
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address)
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or diagnostic data
  • Any video, audio, or image content you record
  • Contacts, calendar, or any other personal data
  • Game scores, team names, player rosters, or any data you enter into the app

All game data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Local Storage

Video, Audio, and Game Data

All video recordings, highlight clips, and exported stats PDFs are stored exclusively on your device in Movies/ScoreFrame. We have no access to these files.

Game scores, team names, colors, rosters, and settings are stored locally as JSON files and SharedPreferences. None of this data leaves your device.

Uninstalling the app removes all app settings and game data. Video recordings in your media storage are managed by you.

Connectivity

Wi-Fi Direct (Remote Scoring)

ScoreFrame's remote scoring connects two phones directly over Wi-Fi Direct. No internet, router, or external server is involved. Data transmitted includes only scoring commands, sport state updates, and optionally a camera preview stream. No personal information is exchanged.

Connectivity

Bluetooth (External Button)

ScoreFrame can use a paired Bluetooth device to control recording hands-free. The app listens for key events from devices already paired through your phone's system settings. ScoreFrame does not scan for or manage Bluetooth connections. The registered button's device name and key code are stored locally.

Advertising

Google AdMob Ads

The free tier displays advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising ID, approximate location, device info, ad interaction data, and IP address. This data is collected by Google under their privacy policy. Pro subscribers do not see ads.

Opt out of personalized ads via your device settings under Google > Ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Purchases

Google Play Billing

ScoreFrame offers an optional Pro subscription through Google Play Billing. All transactions are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Third Parties

Third-Party Services

ScoreFrame integrates with Google AdMob (advertising, free tier) and Google Play Billing (purchases). Beyond these, we do not use any analytics, crash reporting, social platforms, or data brokers. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

Feedback

In-App Feedback

The feedback feature opens your device's email app with a pre-filled message. We do not send feedback automatically. Device info is only included if you check the option and send the email yourself.

Children

Children's Privacy

ScoreFrame is not directed at children under 13. Since ScoreFrame collects no personal information from any user, there is no mechanism for a child's data to be gathered.

Retention

Data Retention

ScoreFrame does not retain any user data on external servers. All data is local. Uninstalling removes all app data.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and will revise the date at the top. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
