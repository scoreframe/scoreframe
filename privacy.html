ScoreFrame – Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026  |  Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

ScoreFrame does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All recordings stay on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud uploads, and no tracking.

This Privacy Policy describes how ScoreFrame ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our Android application. We built ScoreFrame to help you record sports video with professional scoreboard overlays — your data stays yours.

Permissions

Permissions We Request and Why

ScoreFrame requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Camera — Used to record video footage and display a live viewfinder with scoreboard overlay. Video is processed on-device using OpenGL ES and saved directly to your local storage. Camera settings (focus, stabilization, white balance, exposure) are controlled locally and never transmitted.
  • Microphone / Audio Recording — Used to capture game audio alongside video. Audio is encoded locally into your recordings and is never transmitted externally. A mute toggle is available to disable microphone capture.
  • Storage (Read & Write) — Used to save recorded video files and highlight clips to your device's Movies/ScoreFrame folder, and to access existing recordings for highlight extraction and stats export (PDF).
  • Wi-Fi / Network State — Used for the remote scoring feature, which connects two phones directly over Wi-Fi Direct. This is a peer-to-peer connection between your devices — no internet, router, or external server is involved. Wi-Fi state is also checked to facilitate Wi-Fi Direct peer discovery.
  • Nearby Devices / Wi-Fi Direct — Used to discover and connect to a second phone running ScoreFrame for remote scoring. The connection is local and direct — no data passes through any external server.
  • Modify Audio Settings — Used for the microphone mute feature during recording.
  • Internet Access — Used only for displaying advertisements (Google AdMob) on the free tier and for processing in-app purchases (Google Play Billing). ScoreFrame does not upload any of your content, recordings, or personal data to the internet.
  • Vibration — Used for haptic feedback on scoring actions and external button events.

Permissions are requested only when you first use the relevant feature and are never used for any purpose beyond what is described above.

Data Collection

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information (ScoreFrame has no accounts)
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address) — we do not read or store these
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or diagnostic data
  • Any video, audio, or image content you record
  • Contacts, calendar, or any other personal data
  • Game scores, team names, player rosters, or any data you enter into the app

All game data (scores, team configurations, player rosters, sport settings) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage or SharedPreferences. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

Local Storage

Video, Audio, and Game Data

All video recordings, audio tracks, extracted highlight clips, and exported stats PDFs are stored exclusively on your device in your local media storage (Movies/ScoreFrame). We have no access to these files.

Game scores, team names, team colors, player rosters, match history, and app settings are stored locally on your device. Sport settings are stored as JSON files in the app's private directory. Preferences are stored in Android SharedPreferences. None of this data leaves your device.

You are in full control of your data. You may delete recordings at any time through the app or your device's file manager. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored settings and game data.

Connectivity

Wi-Fi Direct (Remote Scoring)

ScoreFrame's remote scoring feature allows two phones to connect directly over Wi-Fi Direct for wireless score control. This connection is peer-to-peer — the phones communicate directly with each other without any internet connection, router, or external server.

Data transmitted over Wi-Fi Direct includes only scoring commands, sport state updates, and optionally a camera preview stream (Pro feature). No personal information is exchanged. The connection is temporary and exists only while both phones are actively using the remote scoring feature.

Network Service Discovery (NSD) is used to help the phones find each other on the local network. This broadcasts only the app's service name — no personal or device-identifying information.

Connectivity

Bluetooth (External Button)

ScoreFrame can optionally use a paired Bluetooth device (such as a shutter remote or clicker) to control recording hands-free. The app listens for standard key events from Bluetooth HID devices that are already paired through your phone's system settings.

ScoreFrame does not scan for, pair, or manage Bluetooth connections. It only receives button press events from devices you have already connected to your phone. The registered button's device name and key code are stored locally in app preferences. No Bluetooth data is transmitted externally.

Advertising

Google AdMob Ads

The free tier of ScoreFrame displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising platform operated by Google LLC. To serve ads, AdMob may collect and use certain information from your device, which may include:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Approximate location (country or region, derived from IP address)
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • IP address

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. We do not receive or have access to this data. ScoreFrame Pro subscribers do not see ads, and AdMob is not active for Pro users.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps .

Purchases

Google Play Billing

ScoreFrame offers an optional Pro subscription through Google Play Billing. All purchase transactions are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information (credit card numbers, billing addresses, etc.).

Subscription status is managed by Google Play and queried locally by the app to determine which features to unlock. Your purchase history is governed by Google's Privacy Policy .

Third Parties

Third-Party Services

ScoreFrame integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising on the free tier (see Advertising section above)
  • Google Play Billing — In-app purchase processing (see Purchases section above)

Beyond these two Google services, ScoreFrame does not integrate with any other third-party SDKs, analytics services, crash reporting tools, social platforms, or data brokers. No data is shared with or sold to any third party by us.

Feedback

In-App Feedback

ScoreFrame includes an optional feedback feature that opens your device's email app with a pre-filled message addressed to our support email. We do not send feedback automatically — you must choose to send the email yourself through your own email app.

If you opt to include device information with your feedback, basic details (device model, Android version, app version) are appended to the email body. This information is only sent if you explicitly check the "Include device info" option and then send the email.

Children

Children's Privacy

ScoreFrame is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since ScoreFrame does not collect any personal information from any user, there is no mechanism through which a child's data could be inadvertently gathered.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through our feedback email, please contact us so we can address it promptly.

Retention

Data Retention

ScoreFrame does not retain any user data on external servers. All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all app settings, game data, team configurations, and rosters. Video recordings saved to your device's media storage are managed by you and are not affected by uninstalling the app.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
