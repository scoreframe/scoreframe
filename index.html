Features

Everything You Need at the Game

Built for parents, coaches, players, and content creators who want professional-looking game footage without the production crew.

🎥

Live Scoreboard Overlay

The scoreboard renders in real time using OpenGL ES compositing. It's part of the video frame from the moment you record — not added in post.

📱

Remote Scoring

A second phone controls the score wirelessly over Wi-Fi Direct. No internet, no router needed. One person films, the other keeps score from the stands.

⭐

Automatic Highlights

Tap the star button during recording to mark a moment. ScoreFrame extracts a clip around it automatically — no scrubbing through hours of footage.

🖥️

Full-Screen Mode

Expand the video to fill the entire screen. Scoring controls slide out from the right when you need them. Perfect for tripod setups.

🎮

Bluetooth Button Control

Pair any Bluetooth clicker to start, pause, and capture highlights without touching the phone. Mount it and control from the sideline.

📡

Live Streaming

Stream your game live to YouTube with one tap. Scoreboard included in the broadcast. Share a link with family who can't make it to the game.

☁️

Cloud Sync & Game History

Create an optional account to save your teams, rosters, and game history across devices. Track career stats per player — bowling averages, batting stats, win-loss records.

🔒

Your Data, Your Control

Videos never leave your phone. Cloud accounts are optional — the app works fully offline. If you sign in, we store only your teams, rosters, and game stats. You can delete your account and all cloud data at any time.

How It Works

Three Steps. That's It.

1

Pick Your Sport

Choose from 12 sports. Each has a purpose-built scoreboard with the right stats for that game.

2

Hit record and tap to update the score. The scoreboard updates live on the video in real time.

3

Share

Your video is done — scoreboard burned in, highlights clipped. Share straight from your gallery.

  • No accounts on either phone
  • Controller phone is always free
  • Works in gyms with no signal
