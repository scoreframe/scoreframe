Features

Everything You Need at the Game

Built for parents, coaches, players, and content creators who want professional-looking game footage without the production crew.

🎥

Live Scoreboard Overlay

The scoreboard renders in real time using OpenGL ES compositing. It's part of the video frame from the moment you record — not added in post.

📱

Remote Scoring

Connect a second phone or invite someone over the internet to control the score while you film. Wi-Fi, Wi-Fi Direct, Bluetooth, or web browser — whatever works.

⭐

Highlights & Buffer Mode

Tap the star during recording to clip a highlight. Or use Buffer Mode — a rolling 60-second buffer that only saves the moments you choose. No full game video needed.

📺

Live Streaming

Stream your game live to YouTube with the scoreboard burned into the broadcast. Share a link with family who can't make it. Score updates appear for viewers in real time.

🏠

Team Hubs

Create a public or private team page with schedules, rosters, game history, and live stream links. Fans follow your team and get notified when you go live.

⌚

Wear OS Companion

Score from your wrist. The watch app shows a live scoreboard, lets you enter scores, and control recording. Custom bowling scoreboard with frame-by-frame entry.

📹

PiP Camera Overlay

The controller phone's camera streams a live picture-in-picture overlay onto the recorded video. Point it at the venue scoreboard and it's burned right into the footage.

🎮

Bluetooth Button Control

Pair any Bluetooth clicker to start, pause, and capture highlights without touching the phone. Mount it on a tripod and control from the sideline.

☁️

Cloud Sync & Game History

Create an optional account to save your teams, rosters, and game history across devices. Track career stats per player — bowling averages, batting stats, win-loss records.

🔍

Quick Settings Panel

One-tap access to all controls: resolution, HDR, stabilization, fullscreen mode, scoreboard visibility, score-only mode, and more. Android control-center style.

How It Works

Three Steps. That's It.

1

Pick Your Sport

Choose from 12 sports. Each has a purpose-built broadcast-style scoreboard with the right stats for that game.

2

Record & Score

Hit record and tap to update the score. The scoreboard updates live on the video in real time. Or connect a second phone to keep score remotely.

3

Share

Your video is done — scoreboard burned in, highlights clipped. Share straight from your gallery or stream live to YouTube.

Remote Scoring

Two Phones. Four Ways to Connect.

Connect a second phone nearby or invite someone over the internet to keep score while you focus on filming. They don't even need the app — web scoring works from any browser.

  • Wi-Fi — same network or mobile hotspot
  • Wi-Fi Direct — no router or internet needed
  • Bluetooth — works everywhere, even with no signal
  • Internet — score from anywhere via web browser
  • Controller phone is always free
