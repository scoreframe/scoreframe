ScoreFrame – Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 23, 2026  |  Last updated: February 24, 2026

Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

ScoreFrame does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All recordings stay on your device.

This Privacy Policy describes how ScoreFrame ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our Android application. We built ScoreFrame to help you capture and manage your sports recordings — your data stays yours.

Permissions

Permissions We Request and Why

ScoreFrame requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Camera — Used to record gameplay footage and display a live viewfinder. Video is processed on-device and saved directly to your local storage.
  • Microphone / Audio Recording — Used to capture game audio alongside video. Audio is encoded locally into your recordings and is never transmitted externally.
  • Storage (Read & Write) — Used to save recorded video files to your device and to access existing recordings for playback and highlight extraction.

Permissions are requested only when you first use the relevant feature and are never used for any purpose beyond what is described above.

Data Collection

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not directly collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address)
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or diagnostic data
  • Any video, audio, or image content you record
  • Contacts, calendar, or any other personal data

Note: Our advertising partner Google AdMob may collect certain device information as described in the Advertising section below. This data is collected by Google, not by ScoreFrame.

Local Storage

Video and Audio Files

All video recordings, audio tracks, and extracted highlight clips are stored exclusively on your device in your local media storage. We have no access to these files.

You are in full control of your recordings. You may delete them at any time through the app or your device's file manager. We do not back up, sync, or upload your content to any server.

Advertising

Google AdMob Ads

ScoreFrame displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising platform operated by Google LLC. To serve relevant ads, AdMob may collect and use certain information from your device, which may include:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Approximate location (country or region)
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • IP address

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. We do not receive or have access to this data. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps .

Third Parties

Third-Party Services

Beyond Google AdMob, ScoreFrame does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, analytics services, crash reporting tools, or social platforms. No data is shared with or sold to any third party by us.

Children

Children's Privacy

ScoreFrame is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us so we can address it promptly.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
